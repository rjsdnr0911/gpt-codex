# Codex Craft Audio Attribution

Bundled sound effects are CC0 files selected from Kenney packs and renamed in `public/audio`.

## Kenney Interface Sounds 1.0

- Source: https://opengameart.org/content/interface-sounds
- Creator/distributor: Kenney, https://www.kenney.nl
- License: Creative Commons Zero, CC0
- Used for: UI clicks, confirmations, errors, open/close feedback, block break/place accents.

## Kenney RPG Audio 1.0

- Source: https://opengameart.org/content/50-rpg-sound-effects
- Creator/distributor: Kenney Vleugels, https://www.kenney.nl
- License: Creative Commons Zero, CC0
- Used for: footsteps, pickup, bow/weapon, chest, eating, mob hit/death accents.

## Procedural Audio

Portal, explosion, dragon hit/death accents, and dragon fight ambience are generated in-browser with WebAudio in `src/game/audioSystem.ts`.
